How It Works

Step 1.Intro Call

Every program begins with a phone call. We'll discuss
your objectives, ask a few questions, and maybe even
throw around a few ideas to begin. We'll explain what
we actually do, how it all works, and how we might be
able to help. From there, we'll send you a summary by
e-mail and if all looks good - get started on a proposal.

Step 2. Research & PRoposal
(Complimentary)

We're firm believers in knowing who you're dealing with
before a single dime gets spent. Based on our initial
conversations, we'll start digging through our
mountains of resources to prepare a complimentary
proposal for you, outlining at least 3-5 destinations with
hotels, activities, and other elements we think would
add some color to each option.

Based on the proposed options we'll ask you to narrow
down your top 3 choices and we will get to work
preparing an agenda and high-level prelim budget for
you to compare options and make an informed
decision.

Step 3.Letter OF Engagement

After seeing our proposal, you'll decide whether you
want to engage us to plan your program. Should you
wish to dive deeper or change the scope of the initial
proposal we will ask you to sign a simple agreement
and make a small deposit as a virtual handshake and
acknowledgement of the additional work.

A signed letter of engagement will entitle you to 3
additional scope changes including; date changes, date
pattern changes, destination, hotel options, change in
group size, budget increases/ decrease, changes
thereafter will incur an additional agreed-upon fee
charged per hour.

From there, we'll get cracking on diving deeper to find
+the perfect place for your program.

Step 4.Contract & Planning

By this stage, we've more or less figured out (A) the
destination, (B) where (and when) you're going, and (C)
the general outline of the day-to-day agenda. Only after you're happy with the budget and itinerary
from Step 3, will we finalize the process by signing a
contract. After that, the real fun begins as we roll our
sleeves up and get elbow deep into the heaps of
planning that lies ahead. (But don't worry, we'll be doing
most of it!) 0

Step 5.On-Site Exucution

Our staff will be in the forefront (while simultaneously
in the background, clipboards and all), ensuring
everything runs seamlessly and your guests' every need
and whim is attended to. Think of us as your butler, concierge, and Bat Phone all
rolled into one – except instead of Alfred, call us
Moniker.
